# Addendum: The Cochranblock Community Grant (Philanthropic Tier)

*Place this at the very bottom of your Bespoke Web Appliance Pricing Matrix, clearly separated from the commercial tiers.*

---

At Cochranblock LLC, we believe enterprise-grade security and bespoke engineering should not be entirely locked behind corporate budgets. To support Dundalk and the greater Baltimore community, we offer a heavily subsidized deployment tier strictly reserved for organizations operating for the public good.

## The Community Appliance Grant: $500 (Deployment Fee Only)

- **The Value:** This represents an 85% subsidy of our standard $3,500 Option A Software Appliance.

- **The Deliverable:** A compiled, zero-dependency Rust web appliance (up to 3 core pages) installed directly onto the organization's existing local hardware, fully secured via Cloudflare Zero Trust routing.

- **Eligibility Requirements:** This tier is strictly limited to verified 501(c)(3) non-profit organizations, local public community initiatives, or select veteran-owned micro-businesses in the Baltimore area.

- **Availability Cap:** To ensure our standard enterprise clients receive undivided attention, Cochranblock LLC limits this grant to one (1) deployment per fiscal quarter.

- **Scope Constraints:** This tier includes the core application build and deployment only. Advanced custom logic, integrated payment gateways, and Experimental AI integrations are excluded from the subsidized tier, but may be added at their standard a la carte rates.
