# User Story & Feature Gap Analysis — Skills-Based Hiring

## User Story Analysis

**Primary persona:** Hiring manager / technical recruiter at a skills-based company (defense contractor, cyber firm, product company)

| ID | User Story | Acceptance Criteria |
|----|------------|---------------------|
| US1 | As a hiring manager, I want to quickly see if this candidate has the skills we need | Skills visible within 5 seconds of landing |
| US2 | As a hiring manager, I want to see proof of their work | Portfolio projects, code samples, measurable outcomes |
| US3 | As a hiring manager, I want to understand their fit for our mission | Clear value prop, outcomes, culture alignment |
| US4 | As a hiring manager, I want to contact them easily | One-click email with hiring-relevant subject |
| US5 | As a hiring manager, I want to download their resume | PDF download available |
| US6 | As a hiring manager, I want to know they're open to opportunities | Explicit "Open to work" or "Seeking" signal |
| US7 | As a hiring manager, I want to share this with my team | Clean URL, printable, shareable |
| US8 | As a hiring manager, I want to verify their LinkedIn | LinkedIn link prominent |

## Feature Gap Analysis

| Gap | Current State | Target State | Priority |
|-----|---------------|--------------|----------|
| G1 | Hero: "Get in Touch" / "View Resume" | Primary CTA: "Hire Me" or "Open to Opportunities" | High |
| G2 | No "Open to work" signal | Add "Seeking full-time roles" or similar | High |
| G3 | Email subject: "Consultation Inquiry" | Add "Hiring Inquiry" as primary for job context | High |
| G4 | Skills buried in About > Credentials tab | Skills visible on home (compact section) | High |
| G5 | No resume PDF download | Add "Download Resume (PDF)" link | High |
| G6 | Contact: "Consulting services launching soon" | Reframe for hiring: "Open to full-time and contract roles" | Medium |
| G7 | Hero note: "Resume & consulting — services launching soon" | "Open to full-time roles in cyber & systems engineering" | Medium |
| G8 | Single testimonial | Keep; add "— Available for new opportunities" | Low |

## Recommendations (Apply)

1. **Hero**: Add "Open to full-time roles" note; change primary CTA to "Hire Me" (links to contact)
2. **Hero note**: Replace "services launching soon" with "Open to opportunities in cyber & systems engineering"
3. **Skills on home**: Add compact skills strip below hero stats
4. **Contact**: Update copy for hiring context; email subject "Hiring Inquiry"
5. **Resume PDF**: Add PDF to assets, serve at /assets/resume.pdf, add "Download Resume" link
6. **Footer**: Update "Get in Touch" mailto subject to "Hiring Inquiry"
