Appendix A · Mechanics

How money and IP actually flow

  • IP policy: All Rights Reserved by default; Unlicense-equivalent grant when code on government systems
  • Cross-flow GovCo → JointCo: GovCo licenses IP from JointCo at fair-market rates (30–50% of contract value) for federal contract delivery
  • Cross-flow JointCo → Ghostcoded: JointCo licenses Ghostcoded brand/audience for joint commercial product distribution at fair-market rates
  • Free starter kit Michael → Ghostcoded: Michael's existing consumer products (wowasticker, pocket-server, call-shield, pixel-forge, whyyoulying, illbethejudgeofthat) transfer to Ghostcoded via perpetual royalty-free exclusive license - Tanner gets working portfolio to grow Ghostcoded with from day one, no charge
  • SkillBridge pipeline: JointCo registers as DoD SkillBridge provider, receives zero-cost cleared-vet labor for 180 days per intake, W-2 conversion path to JointCo (commercial) or GovCo (federal) post-SkillBridge
  • Capability transfer between entities by joint cofounder decision - All Rights Reserved → Unlicense flip on federal delivery requires both founders' signatures

Appendix C · The Math · Railgun, not David-vs-Goliath

3.5x base rate × 5x payout ceiling = 17x expected-value advantage over typical pre-seed

Standard pre-seed: 10–15% success rate, $10–50M payout potential, $1–5M expected founder outcome.

This team's verifiable inputs:

  • Michael velocity: 22 products / 60 days = 11–22x typical solo velocity
  • Capital efficiency: $364 spent = 137–1,370x typical capital efficiency
  • Test density: ~1,598 tests at TRL 5 = 8–32x typical density at higher tier
  • Tanner audience: 4K LinkedIn + 74 paid Skool + $100K/7mo inbound = $200–500K + 2–4 years to replicate from scratch
  • John credentials: Zynga IPO + multi-time 0→1 + patents + expert witness = structurally under-priced strategic asset at 1% + retainer

Compounded probability: ~35–50% likelihood of meaningful outcome (vs 15% baseline). 2–3x typical pre-seed success rate.

Payout ceiling: $100M – $3B enterprise value across three lanes (federal lifetime $5–50M via SBIR ladder + commercial AI $10–100M via audience-led GTM + consumer apps $1–10M aggregate).

Expected value: $35M – $1.5B = 7–30x typical pre-seed expected value.

Iron Man framing: Built the suit in a cave with $364 of scraps. Methodology is the moat. "I do it. Then I do it again."

Railgun framing: Properly calibrated structure + high operator skill + right target selection = one-shot heavy targets (federal contracts, commercial AI deals, consumer app market disruption). Not aimed at hoping to get lucky against a Goliath.

Cloud repatriation scaling math: at typical traffic profiles, sovereign cluster + Cloudflare Tunnel delivers same edge distribution + DDoS + analytics + HTTPS at ~$50/mo flat vs $300–5,000/mo cloud. Cost-per-traffic curve collapses to flat - not just cost-per-compute. ~250–1,000x throughput-per-dollar advantage at scale; ~30–100x lower memory footprint per equivalent function.

Honest caveat: probability multipliers are illustrative, not statistically validated. Federal lane gated by procurement timing. Cofounder relationships fail at industry-typical rates regardless of input quality. 18-month runway buffer assumes no major black swan.
